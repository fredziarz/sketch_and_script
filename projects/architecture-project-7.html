Minimalist Studio Loft

Studio loft designed for an artist, emphasizing clean lines and natural light

Location Gdansk, Poland
Year 2023
Area 45 m²
Duration 2 months

Project Overview

This comprehensive renovation project transformed an outdated 80m² apartment into a modern, functional living space. The design focuses on maximizing natural light, creating an open-plan living area, and incorporating contemporary materials with a warm, minimalist aesthetic.

The client's brief emphasized creating a spacious feel within the compact footprint, integrating smart storage solutions, and maintaining a connection to the building's architectural heritage while embracing modern design principles.
