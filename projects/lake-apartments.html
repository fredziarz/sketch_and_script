Lake Apartments

Residential Development

New estate development almost on the bank of Lake Tegel in Berlin, creating a harmonious blend of modern living spaces and natural waterfront surroundings. The buildings integrate seamlessly with the landscape, offering residents panoramic views of the lake and surrounding nature.

The design emphasizes light-filled interiors and seamless indoor-outdoor connections, with floor-to-ceiling windows that frame the waterfront vistas. Sustainable materials and energy-efficient systems ensure minimal environmental impact while maximizing comfort and livability.

Completed 2020 • Berlin, Germany
