Two Passions, One Purpose

I've always been drawn to creating things—whether it's a perfectly balanced room layout or an elegantly architected codebase. To most people, interior design and software development seem worlds apart. To me, they're two sides of the same coin: both require creative problem-solving, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of how people interact with their environment.

By day, I ensure software works flawlessly through systematic testing and quality engineering. By evening, I design spaces where life works beautifully. This unique combination allows me to bring systematic thinking to design and creative vision to code.
